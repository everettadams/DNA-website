How It Works

1

Phase 1: Transcription

You'll see a DNA template strand with bases A, T, G, and C. Click the correct RNA bases to build the complementary mRNA strand. Remember: A pairs with U (not T in RNA!), T pairs with A, G pairs with C, and C pairs with G.

2

Phase 2: Translation

Your mRNA is divided into codons (groups of 3 bases). Select the correct tRNA anticodon that matches each codon. Each correct match brings an amino acid to build your protein chain!

3

Meet Your Protein

When complete, you'll see what protein you've created! Learn about real proteins like insulin (the key that unlocks cells), hemoglobin (oxygen delivery), or antibodies (body defenders) and what they do in your body.

4

Level Up!

Each protein you complete increases your level and score. Sequences get longer and more challenging as you advance. How many proteins can you create?
